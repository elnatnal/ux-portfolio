Adzerk

Typographic design, Visual Design, Front End Development

An extensive brochure website. Client approached me with wireframes seeking help with the visual aspect of the design. Focusing on typography as the foundation for their content, I aimed to put their colorful content and voice on display. I also developed the site for production using various modern methods to speed up page load and optimize best for future browsers and devices. September 2012

Stealz

Information Architecture, Wireframing, Design

Stealz is (and will be) a location based, sharing coupon application on iOS and Android devices. I lead a small team of developers and another designer in mapping out and planning the application. After quite a few revisions, 50 sticky notes completed the architecture. After the architecture and flow was established, I wireframed out each view. The Creative Director used the wireframes to design two initial views and the identity system. I fleshed out the rest of the views and manipulating designs as development issues came up. Currently producing all the assets for iOS and Android OSes.  January 2013 (expected)

N.C. State College of Design

Information Architecture, Wireframes, Design, Project Manager

The N.C. State College of Design approached us to redesign and redevelop their main marketing site. They provided us with designs students developed over a semester and a rough architecture of how they saw the site being used. We had to design seven templates to be used across the whole site. I did a more developed information architecture through a content audit and defining a loose content strategy. From there, I stepped into wireframing out the seven templates. The Creative Director worked on design of the main feel. From the front page I designed all the inner pages.  September 2012

Clinical Trials

Planning, Wireframes, Design

Clinical Trial Results is an application for clinical trialists to enter failed clinical trial results. This is the only public repository of failed clinical trials in the world. I was tasked to map the workflow, wireframe every view, and design a modular system that would be expanded indefinitely. The modular navigation appends a new tab as the user is assigned an additional role. The forms are the most critical part of the system. Every form was given a large amount of time, testing for ease of use, expediency, and accuracy.  July 2012

Flying Saucer

Planning, Wireframes, Design, Animation, Videography

An iPhone application design for a local bar, The Flying Saucer, for my senior design project at university. Patrons of the bar have the opportunity to join a club where they quest to try 200 of the beers available. Currently, the interaction is obtrusive and an annoyance to the club members. They have to wait in a long line to see what beers they have previously had, pick out the beers they want for the night and print off little receipts to give to the waitress at the end of the night. This application aims to rectify that nuisance and obtrusion. This project was a semifinalist in the Adobe Design Achievement Awards in Mobile Design, 2011. May 2012

Over the past few years I've completed a variety of animations for a few organizations.
